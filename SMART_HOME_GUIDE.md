## ❓ Something Not Working?

1. **HomePod not responding?** — Make sure you're in the living room and say *"Hey Siri"* clearly first
2. **Alexa not responding?** — Look for the orange ring (muted) — press the microphone button on top
3. **Lights won't respond?** — Try the physical switch first, then try again
4. **Ask Darrian** — he built all of this and can fix it in 30 seconds 😄
